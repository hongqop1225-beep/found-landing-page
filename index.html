Why Found exists

We built the opposite
of a dating app.

Swiping made us lonely. Endless text threads that go nowhere. Matches that never meet. Profiles that feel like résumés. We're all tired of it.

Found is not another dating app. It's a matchmaker — a quiet one that lives on your phone and pays attention to the world around you.

When you open Found, you tell us who you are, what you value, and what you're really looking for. Our AI learns the shape of your heart — not just your height and hobbies. Then it waits.

And then one day, something magical happens.

You're at your favourite café. You're walking the coast. You're just living your life. Your phone quietly notices someone nearby — someone whose values, humour, and intentions match yours. Not because they swiped right. Because they're actually the one you've been looking for.

They appear in your Waiting Room. You hear their voice before you see their face. You have only a few messages — not hundreds — because we believe real connection happens in person, not on a screen.

Meet in person. Or let them go. No pressure. No endless scrolling. No performing for strangers. Just real life, made a little more magical.

Questions, answered

The honest answers.

Everything you might be wondering before you download.

Is Found really free?

Yes. Your first match is completely free — no trial countdown, no credit card. You can hear voice intros, see who's crossed your path, and unlock your first connection at no cost. Premium plans unlock unlimited matches, AI coaching, and deeper insights.

How does the "crossing paths" feature work?

Found uses your phone's location quietly in the background. When someone whose values, intentions, and personality match yours is physically nearby — at the same café, walking the same beach, on the same train — our AI matchmaker recognises the moment and gently lets you know. No pings, no spam. Just the right person at the right time.

Is my location data private?

Always. Found never shares your exact location with anyone — not other users, not advertisers, never. Matching happens through anonymised proximity signals. Your privacy is the foundation of everything we build.

Why are messages limited?

Because real connection doesn't happen in text threads. We give you just enough words to break the ice — then we trust you to take it offline. No more weeks of back-and-forth that leads nowhere. Found is built to get you across a real table, not a glowing screen.

When can I download Found?

Found is launching first in Manly, Sydney on the App Store — currently in final review. Join the early list below to be the first to know the moment we go live. Android coming next.

What if I'm in a small town, not Sydney?

Found works best where there's density — but everyone is welcome. We're starting in Sydney and rolling out city by city. Sign up below and we'll let you know when Found arrives where you live.

Can I cancel anytime?

Of course. Cancel from the App Store with one tap. No questions, no friction, no guilt. We only want you here if Found is making your life better.
